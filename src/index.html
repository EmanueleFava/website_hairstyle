Located in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique salon specializing in cuts, color, and styling, while also offering top-notch makeup and nail services.

Renowned for its edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts, Hair Day takes pride in creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for every client.

Our philosophy is simple: with our expert cuts, your inner beauty will shine through.

At Hair Day, we bring your inner beauty to life with each style, ensuring you leave feeling confident and beautiful.

Our talented team is dedicated to providing personalized care, using their expertise to unleash your true beauty.
